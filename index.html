GUESS HUE?

Guess who? Nope! It's Guess Hue, the color guessing game.

Playing is simple, you are shown a black and white image and all you have to do it guess the right color.

You know the WHO, now guess the HUE

Select a catergory below to test your intution!



EASY: Guess the color of familiar cartoons!


MEDIUM: Guess the color of well-known logos!


HARD: Guess the color of the car!


CHALLENGE: Guess the color of international unicolored flags!
